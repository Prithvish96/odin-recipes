Troll and Unicorn Stew

Description

A hearty stew that combines the rugged essence of a troll's bone with the delicate flavor of unicorn horn shavings. This magical stew is perfect for a feast fit for fantasy realms, blending mythical strength and enchantment.

Ingredients

  • Troll’s bone
  • Unicorn horn shavings
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Barley
  • Beef broth
  • Bay leaves

Steps

  1. In a large pot, brown the troll’s bone over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in barley and beef broth. Add bay leaves.
  4. Simmer for 1 hour, or until the troll’s bone is tender and the barley is cooked.
  5. Remove the troll’s bone and discard it.
  6. Stir in unicorn horn shavings just before serving to infuse a touch of enchantment.
